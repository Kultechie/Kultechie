Why it Matters

"Skool n Skill 4 Naija" pioneers a transformative approach by seamlessly integrating digital education with life skills, health education, intercultural and peace education, gender equality education, and democratic citizenship education. Through a collaborative effort involving local CSOs, government entities, activists, and informal associations, the project catalyzes a national network. The initiative extends beyond traditional boundaries, hosting a digital skills expo that not only showcases achievements but also fosters community engagement. By empowering local CSOs through a strategic sub-granting scheme, the project ensures sustained impact and community ownership, contributing to a comprehensive educational landscape in Nigeria.

Project Activities

Skool n Skill 4 Naija's impactful activities include launching a nationwide digital skills expo, fostering local CSO engagement, and implementing a sub-granting scheme for sustainable impact. Our focus areas span intercultural education, gender equality, and democratic citizenship, enriching the secondary school experience.

Innovative Approaches

We pioneer dynamic methods in addressing substance abuse in Nigeria. Our innovative approaches include utilizing peer-to-peer influence, conducting awareness campaigns, media dialogues, and advocating policy changes. These strategies bolster our commitment to building a resilient and empowered youth population.
